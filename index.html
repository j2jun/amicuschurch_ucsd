The initial purpose of Amicus Church at UC San Diego was primarily for going into the UCSD campus to create a community/worship space for students to bring the church at the campus where they are. Amicus Church like its name wanted to bring the "friendship" inside the campus. However, this initial purpose did not fully bloom since going into the UCSD campus was not the place where most students were. Then, the real question is where is the fundamental place that Amicus Church needed to meet. The answer to the question could be found in the purpose of friendship. The opposite of friendship is loneliness and that was where Amicus Church has to go and meet the students. How can we meet students at where they are at the state of lonliness? We know that no one can fix the problem yet we can be there and walk alongside with students as friends would do. Friends do not necessarily resolve the problem rather friends give encouragement and support to not give up and continue their journey of life together sharing time and space with one another.
